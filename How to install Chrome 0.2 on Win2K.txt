Make sure you have 7-Zip installed.

Open the installer with 7-Zip by right clicking on the installer and click open archive.

Open inside of Chrome.7z

Extract the Chrome-bin folder to C:\.

Press WinKey + R to type C:\Chrome-bin.

On the Chrome-bin folder window, move chrome.exe to the 0.2.149.27 folder. And start chrome.exe from the 0.2.149.27 folder and will start.
